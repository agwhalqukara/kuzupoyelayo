Who is Nicole Petallides?

Nicole Petallides is an American anchor for the Fox Business Network along with her co-anchor, Jenna Lee. Prior, she worked on Bloomberg Television as well as an assistant producer for CNBC.

What are the birth facts of Nicole Petallides?

Nicole Petallides was born on 20th September 1971, in Queens, New York City, New York, United States. Her birth name is Nicole Anais Petallides. Her ethnicity is Greek and her nationality is American.

Talking about her parents, her mother’s name is Fannie Holliday, and her father’s name is John C. Petallides. Her father is a founder of Amfax company and her mother is a founder of a newspaper company, Perini. She was born in a well-off family. There is no information about her siblings, she seems to be a single child.

Nicole Petallides: Early Professional Life and Career

Nicole Petallides started her career in broadcasting soon after her studies on the Fox-affiliated news morning show, Good Day New York. Gradually, she became the producer of Jones Television.
