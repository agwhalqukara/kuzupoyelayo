Get To Know Woody Harrelson's Wife

August 2024 · 9 minute read

Laura Louie is the wife of Woody Harrelson, a Hollywood star actor whose full name is Woodrow Tracy Harrelson. The couple has been together for over 30 years but they have been married as husband and wife for 14 years. 

Woody gained popularity for his role as Woody Boyd in the comedy series Cheers. He had actually met his future wife Laura Louie while shooting the series. Woody went on to star in numerous motion pictures like Regular Born Killers, Zombieland, The Hunger Games series, and Presently You See Me series to mention but a few. All through his career, Laura was beside him first as an assistant, then partner, wife, and finally the mother of their three beautiful daughters.

Laura along with her husband are staunch vegans and practical environmentalists. While Laura’s entrepreneurial spirit thrives in her growing eco-based empire, her compassion and maturity is evident in how she handles her marriage despite the many challenges she faces as the wife of a celebrity. As a wife, mother, entrepreneur, and philanthropist who flies under the radar, she is more than just Woody Harrelson’s wife, she is his better half and the woman behind his success.

The Onset of Laura Louie and Woody Harrelson’s Relationship Was Dramatic

Laura met Woody Harrelson on the set of Cheers, Woody who was gradually becoming famous for his role in the series had casually asked “Hey, does anybody here have secretarial skills? ‘Cause I could really use an assistant. Laura Louie, who was just 22 at that time, raised her hand. She was visiting the set with a media workshop from UCLA. In fact, she had taken the place of a student who fell sick and couldn’t be part of the team. It seems their meeting was destined to happen after all.

Laura then became Woody’s assistant after an interview with the actor at his place in Marina del Rey. Recalling the interview, Woody says: “We had one of the greatest conversations I ever had, so she became my assistant. She was so great that every other person in Cheers got an assistant after that. She became my assistant the day after I met her and for the next three years.”

Woody Harrelson struggled with his feelings for Laura as he wasn’t ready to admit that he was falling for her. However, when he went to Kenya for Christmas three years later, he found himself ‘fantasising’ about her. On getting back to L.A, and against the advice of his friends, he expressed his feelings to her via a song he wrote. Some lines of the song go thus; “I’m just 28, yet I’m exhausted with all that I do/I don’t have anything when I’m not close to you, will you consider me, baby? Cause I’m considering you”.

They Dated for 20 Years Before Becoming Husband and Wife

Laura, who understood that the song was meant for her, also confessed saying; “Woody, I’ve been in love with you for the last two and a half years,” and thus began their relationship. The two dated for 20 years before officially tying the knot on December 28, 2008, in a very small ceremony near Harrelson’s home in Kipahulu, Maui.

The very simple wedding cost around $500, with Woody saying; “I have found, the least expensive things can be the most personally rewarding. Take my wedding. We didn’t feel the need to shell out a ton of cash and do anything over the top. It was basically just a bunch of good friends getting together in Maui.” The wedding was attended by celebrities like Owen Wilson, Sean Penn, and Willie Nelson. Canadian artist and multi-instrumentalist Alanis Morisette graced the occasion with his beautiful melodies.

How Laura Louie’s Marriage Survived Woody Harrelson’s Cheating Scandal

Before meeting and dating Laura Louie, Woody Harrelson didn’t believe in the concept of marriage. This isn’t surprising as he himself never had an idea of what a healthy, committed relationship should look like as his father abandoned the family and his mom raised him and his brothers alone. Prior to his relationship with Laura, Woody Harrelson was known as a serial womanizer and was even nicknamed “Wicked Woody.” In 1985, he even got married to Neil Simon’s daughter, Nancy Simon. The couple divorced after 10 months, although the marriage was claimed to be a result of a joke that turned sour.

However, all these changed when he met Laura. As far as we know, Woody has not stepped out on his wife until the foursome scandal that happened in 2002. On a trip to London in 2002, Woody claimed that two women came up to him in a bar and they left the bar with another girl whom she didn’t even know was with them. It turned out that the last girl was a journalist who convinced the others to blackmail him and the news of his foursome soon hit the tabs.

Although he was hoping Laura never found out about the scandal, she somehow got to know and though she was upset, Laura forgave Woody after he ‘grovelled’ for forgiveness. Woody explained that Laura was quite compassionate about the whole thing and the couple is very much together. Woody even made a film on his terrible experience in London called Lost in London in 2017.

Laura and Woody Are Parents To 3 Girls They Call “The Goddess Trilogy”

Laura and Woody had first met in 1987, meaning that they have been together for 36 years and counting. It is interesting to note that all their children who are all girls were born before the duo even got married. But this didn’t stop the couple from having a blissful relationship and running their businesses together. Laura Louie and her husband Woody Harrelson fondly refer to their daughters as the goddess trilogy.

The 27 years old, who largely lives a very private life, made an appearance with her father in a short film, U2: Song for Someone in 2015. She also once led a Ted Talk at her high school, where she spoke on the necessity of reducing the dependence on phones.

She appears to be following in her father’s footsteps as she has already made her debut as an actress. She debuted in LBJ in 2016 as a crowd member and in 2017 she played the role of Deni in Lost in London.

Laura Louie Built Her Career Alongside Woody Harrelson

Laura started off her career working as a personal assistant for her husband Woody Harrelson. When the two got together, she left the job of an assistant. In the mid-’90s, she co-founded Yoganics, an organic grocery delivery service with her friend Konda Mason. Woody Harrelson, who was by then her live-in partner, helped in financing the business.

Yoganics started off small, but by 1996, it had grown to make deliveries of over 300 boxes every week. Yoganics has attracted select clientele like Brad Pitt, Helen Hunt, and Oliver Stone. With Yoganics, Laura hopes to promote healthy and clean eating, as well as make organic produce easily accessible to people.

Laura Louie, along with her husband, also started a company in 2001 called Voice Yourself, which aims to encourage people to embrace sustainable living. The website offers diet advice and vegan recipes using fresh produce as well as encourages people to protest pollution. They also hope to inspire a global movement that encourages organic living and restores harmony and balance to earth.

Laura, along with her husband, is vegan and the Eco-Village project is a physical manifestation of the couple’s advocacy for eco-friendly lifestyle and green initiatives. The beautiful family of five, along with two dogs and cats, live in the Eco-Village located in Mauli, Hawaii, with roughly 200 people. The Eco-Village is designed as a self-sufficient community that lives a simple eco-friendly lifestyle.

Laura Louie has no social media presence as she has chosen to stay out of the limelight. The glimpse she affords the media are only when she is spending time with her family on vacation or gracing the red carpet with her husband, Woody Harrelson.
