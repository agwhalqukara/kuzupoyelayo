Chris Young Extends Im Comin Over Tour Dates Inside! Celeb Secrets Country

July 2024 · 3 minute read

It’s official! Chris Young has extended his I’m Comin’ Over Tour by adding 25 shows to his calendar! The new tour dates begin on September 29th in Bakersfield, CA, and finish on December 10th in Lexington, KY.

Cassadee Pope, who will be on the road with him this summer, will also be a special guest at all on these fall tour dates. In addition to Pope, Dan + Shay will be hitting the stage with Young this September and October, while Dustin Lynch opens the November and December shows.

“I’ve been friends with Dan + Shay and Dustin Lynch for a while now,” Young says in regards to the new fall dates, “so to have them join me on tour and to have Cassadee back out with us again, it’s going to be a blast.”

You can find all of Chris Young’s newly announced tour dates down below! Is he coming to a city near you? If so, you don’t want to miss this!
