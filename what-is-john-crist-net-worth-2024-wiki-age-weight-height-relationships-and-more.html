The Rise of a Talent

John Crist’s journey as an elite stand-up talent began modestly in Atlanta, Georgia. Born to a pastor, Crist’s upbringing was steeped in Christian culture, a theme that would later heavily influence his comedy.

He first dipped his toes into comedy waters during open mic nights at a Chili’s restaurant, a humble beginning for what would become an illustrious career.

After graduating from Samford University with a journalism degree, Crist took the plunge into the comedy scene.

His early performances at local comedy clubs and churches laid the groundwork for his distinctive style, merging observational humor with Christian and millennial themes.

This unique angle in comedy not only resonated with a wide audience but also set the stage for his future successes.

Crist’s big break came through his YouTube channel, where sketches like Every Parent at Disney and Millennial International went viral.
