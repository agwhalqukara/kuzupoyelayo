Julianna Rose Mauriello's biography

Mauriello was born into a family with a performing arts background, and the idea fascinated her at a very young age. She watched her elder siblings performing in various school plays and theatre performances, encouraging her to pursue the course at a tender age.

Where is Julianna Rose Mauriello from?

The American actress was born in Irvington, New York. Her older sister, Alex Mauriello, is an actress who has appeared in Casting Call, Daytime Divas and Still the King.

Education

She first attended the Performing Arts School in New York City, then pursued her undergraduate education at Middlebury College, earning her degree.

She later pursued a master's in occupational therapy at the Columbia University Vagelos College of Physicians and Surgeons.

Acting career

Julianna Rose Mauriello from Middlebury started professional acting in 2002, when she signed a contract with her first agent, Nancy Carson. Her first role was as Lil Titch in the revival of a Rodgers & Hammerstein classic, Oklahoma.

She has also made commercials and speciality films, working with Disney Entertainment, Volvo, Wendy's, and AstraZeneca (Nexium).
